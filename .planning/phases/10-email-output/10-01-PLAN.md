<objective>
Fix MJML compilation and enforce absolute URLs in email output.

Purpose: The GrapesJS editor currently returns raw MJML XML from `editor.getHtml()`, which gets stored in `content_html` and sent directly to SES — email clients cannot render MJML. Additionally, no guard exists to catch relative image/link URLs that would break in email clients.

Output: Editor compiles MJML to HTML on save; worker has defensive guards for uncompiled MJML and relative URLs; unit tests verify guards.
</objective>
